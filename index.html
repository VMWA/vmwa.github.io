How I work

Most of my work takes one of two forms: a bounded project or an ongoing retainer. Either works for me. What matters is that we align early on what "done" means and how we stay in sync when the scope or priorities change. I work primarily remote.

Project work

A clear goal with a clear end: an MVP, an internal tool, or a defined piece of a product. I scope it, build it, and hand over something that runs in production.

Ongoing / retainer

A set amount of time each month for features, fixes, and whatever comes up. Useful when you need steady engineering help without hiring full time yet.

In both cases I own the technical decisions and the implementation. I use AI coding tools as leverage in day-to-day development. They speed things up, but the judgment stays with me. If a product needs AI features, I add them when they clearly help users, not by default.

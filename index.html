Let's build.

Whether you're launching an MVP, scaling a prototype, or building critical features, different situations call for different approaches, from hands-on coding to strategic thinking. I work with companies in two flexible ways:

Project Consultant: When you have a clear scope and timeline. I deliver focused, hands-on development for defined projects or critical feature launches, from design to production deployment.

Fractional Tech Lead: When you need ongoing technical leadership without full-time overhead. I combine active coding with architecture decisions, roadmap planning, and AI integration.

Both models share the same principles: clear communication throughout, thoughtful solutions, and a focus on what you actually need, whether that's a quick prototype or a full-scale system.
